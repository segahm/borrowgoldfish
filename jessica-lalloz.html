Created by the highly talented, Jessica Lalloz, this restaurant is a story to be told. The food is a mixture of French cuisine with a twist of Guatemalo-americano roots. No day passes by without a unique item on a menu. It is truly a one place you can go to and try something new everyday. 99% of customers never get tired of visiting Jessica's restaurant a few times a week.

A few restaurants are similar to this one - namely: Cali Sas Mexican in Apple Valley and Johnny Russo's Italian Kitchen in Banning, California. That said, no restaurant even comes close to what Jessica offers.
